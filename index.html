Protecting Papua New Guinea's Sovereignty

The Papua New Guinea Defence Force is committed to defending our nation's territory, contributing to regional stability, and assisting civilian authorities in times of need.

Learn More Join Us
🛡️

Our Mission

To defend Papua New Guinea and its territories against external attack and provide assistance in meeting national emergencies.

⚓

Maritime Element

Protecting our vast maritime domain and exclusive economic zone from illegal fishing and transnational crime.

✈️

Air Element

Providing aerial surveillance, transport, and support operations across Papua New Guinea's challenging terrain.

👥

Land Element

The backbone of PNGDF, responsible for ground operations, border security, and nation-building tasks.
