Latest News

28 April 2026 Operations

PNGDF Completes Joint Border Patrol Operation

The PNGDF has successfully completed a comprehensive joint patrol operation along the country's western border. Combining Land and Air Elements, Operation Border Shield 2026 involved over 200 personnel conducting surveillance, community engagement, and security assessments across border regions. The operation reaffirms PNGDF's commitment to maintaining territorial integrity and border security.

15 April 2026 Maritime

New Guardian-Class Patrol Boat Commissioned

The PNGDF Maritime Element today commissioned its newest Guardian-class patrol boat, HMPNGS Kieta, at Lombrum Naval Base. The vessel will strengthen PNG's capacity to patrol its vast Exclusive Economic Zone, combat illegal fishing, and support search and rescue operations. The commissioning ceremony was attended by Defence Minister Dr. Billy Joseph and Commander PNGDF Rear Admiral Philip Polewara.

02 April 2026 Recruitment

2026 PNGDF Recruitment Drive Now Open

The Papua New Guinea Defence Force has officially opened its 2026 recruitment intake. Applications are being accepted online through the official recruitment portal. Positions are available across all three service elements — Land, Maritime, and Air. The PNGDF is seeking motivated Papua New Guineans who meet the eligibility criteria to serve their nation.

10 March 2026 Training

PNGDF Officers Complete International Training Program

A cohort of 15 PNGDF officers have returned after completing an intensive international training program focused on command and leadership. The program, conducted in partnership with the Australian Defence Force, included modules on operational planning, strategic leadership, and joint operations coordination.

22 February 2026 Community

PNGDF Engineers Complete School Infrastructure Project

PNGDF engineer personnel have completed construction of two new classroom buildings at a primary school in Central Province as part of the force's nation-building program. The project is part of ongoing efforts by the PNGDF to support community development and education infrastructure in rural areas.
