Careers

Serve your nation — build a rewarding career with the PNG Defence Force

Why Join the PNGDF?

A career in the Papua New Guinea Defence Force offers unique opportunities to serve your country while developing lifelong skills and experiences. Whether you serve in the Land, Maritime, or Air Element, you will receive world-class training, competitive remuneration, and the opportunity to travel and work alongside partner nations.

Career Pathways

Officer Entry

For university graduates and school leavers with suitable qualifications.

  • Officer Cadet Training Program (12 months)
  • Staff College and Command training
  • Leadership and management development
  • Opportunities for international training

Soldier/Sailor/Airman Entry

For individuals seeking entry-level enlistment in the PNGDF.

  • Basic Recruit Training (16 weeks)
  • Corps-specific trade training
  • Career progression to senior ranks
  • Specialist skill development

Specialist Entry

For professionals with specialised skills and qualifications.

  • Medical and dental professionals
  • Engineers and technical specialists
  • Legal and administrative professionals
  • Information technology specialists

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for recruitment into the PNGDF, applicants must meet the following minimum criteria:

  • Be a citizen of Papua New Guinea
  • Be between 18 and 27 years of age (officer applicants up to 30)
  • Have a minimum Grade 12 certificate with passes in English and Mathematics
  • Be medically and physically fit
  • Have no criminal record
  • Meet minimum height requirements (165cm for males, 155cm for females)

Benefits

Competitive Salary

Attractive remuneration packages with annual increments

Free Medical & Dental

Comprehensive healthcare for members and families

Housing Assistance

Subsidised accommodation or housing allowances

Education & Training

Ongoing professional development and study opportunities

Retirement benefits through the Defence Force superannuation scheme

Travel Opportunities

Domestic and international travel as part of your service

How to Apply

The PNGDF recruitment process is managed online through the official recruitment portal. Applications for the 2026 intake are now open.

1

Visit the official recruitment portal at recruit.defence.gov.pg

2

Create an account and complete the online application form

3

Upload required documents (certificates, identification, medical records)

4

Attend selection testing and interview if shortlisted

5

Undergo medical and fitness assessments

6

Report for basic training upon successful selection
